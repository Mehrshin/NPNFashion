FAQ



What is the purpose of the site?

• The primary purpose of every ecommerce website is to make profit. So, increasing sale is what we look for in the first place. The other purpose is to improve customer satisfaction which leads to better sale rates.


Who is the intended audience?

• Fashion-oriented people, shopaholics, and high-school aged consumers looking for latest fashion trends are among the target audience of our website.


What opportunity, problem, or issue is your site addressing?

• To bring a new perspective on clothing to the customer’s eyes by showing them how our clothing choices impact our lives.


What do you want the web site to accomplish?

• Attract more audience (potential customers) and turn them into customers.
